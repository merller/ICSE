Most Detailed Level
When an intrusion is detected, turn on the lights, play a warning sound, start camera recording, send an alert to the security company, and check if all doors and windows are closed. If any doors or windows are open, send a warning message. When safety is confirmed, turn off the lights, stop camera recording, and stop the warning sound.

Medium Level Description
When an intrusion is detected, turn on the lights, play a warning sound, start recording, send an alert, and check the doors and windows. When safety is confirmed, turn off the lights, stop recording, and stop the warning sound.

Simplified Level Description
Respond to an intrusion by turning on lights, playing a warning sound, recording, sending an alert, and checking security. Respond to safety confirmation by turning off lights and stopping alarms and recording.
