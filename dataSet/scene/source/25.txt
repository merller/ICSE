Most Detailed Level
When flight information is updated, log the new flight information and send a security check reminder and a boarding notification. For baggage handling, send a baggage drop reminder, track the baggage status, and send a baggage arrival notification. Monitor and adjust the environment by logging and updating temperature, humidity, and air quality. For passenger comfort services, play background music and send a reminder for charging stations.

Medium Level Description
When flight information is updated, log the new flight info and send security check and boarding notifications. For baggage handling, send a baggage drop reminder, track baggage, and send a baggage arrival notification. Monitor and adjust the environment by logging and updating environmental data. For passenger comfort services, play background music and send a charging station reminder.

Simplified Level Description
Update flight information, handle baggage, monitor and adjust the environment, and provide passenger comfort services by playing music and sending reminders based on detected events.
