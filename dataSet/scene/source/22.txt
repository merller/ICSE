Most Detailed Level
When a car is entering, turn on the garage lights, recognize the license plate, and update the parking availability. When a car is exiting, recognize the license plate, process the payment for the recognized license plate, update the parking availability, and turn off the garage lights.

Medium Level Description
When a car is entering, turn on the garage lights, recognize the license plate, and update parking availability. When a car is exiting, recognize the license plate, process the payment, update the parking availability, and turn off the garage lights.

Simplified Level Description
Control garage lights, recognize license plates, update parking availability, and process payments based on car entering and exiting events.
