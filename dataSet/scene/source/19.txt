Most Detailed Level
When the race starts, start the race timer, play race background music, and start the live stream. When a score is recorded for team A or team B, update the scoreboard with the respective score and team name. When the race ends, stop the race timer, stop the race background music, and stop the live stream.

Medium Level Description
When the race starts, start the timer, play music, and start the live stream. When a score is recorded for team A or team B, update the scoreboard. When the race ends, stop the timer, stop the music, and stop the live stream.

Simplified Level Description
Control the timer, music, live stream, and scoreboard based on the start and end of the race and scoring events.
